Louisville Campus

Sullivan’s main campus is located in Louisville, Kentucky – a city with unlimited cultural and professional opportunities, including the area’s most diverse communities of restaurants, clubs and retail stores.

Lexington Campus

This innovative campus is designed to help you develop real-world experience and the advanced training you’ll need to succeed.

Fort Knox Extension

Recognized as a military-friendly school, Sullivan University is proud to have served the Fort Knox community for over 30 years. In addition to serving active duty service members, veterans and their families, our full-service campus is also open to the local community.
